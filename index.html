Welcome to Sprout & Grow

Your Local Source for Healthy, Thriving Plants

At Sprout & Grow, we’re passionate about helping you build vibrant gardens. Whether you’re a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our nursery offers expert advice and hand-picked plants that flourish. For more gardening inspiration, check out Gardener’s Supply.

We focus on native plants and easy-care options, so your greenery thrives even with a busy schedule. Our horticulturists are available to offer personalized guidance.

Beyond plants, we host workshops and offer seasonal garden kits. Stop by to learn, explore, and connect with fellow plant enthusiasts.
